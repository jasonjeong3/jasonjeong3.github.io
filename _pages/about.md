I am a Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering at the [Visual Intelligence Lab (VILab)](https://vi.kaist.ac.kr/) at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), advised by Prof. Kuk-Jin Yoon. I received my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from KAIST and my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering (minor in Computer Science) from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

My research is in **computer vision**, with a focus on **omnidirectional (360°) imaging**, **dual-pixel cameras**, and **vision-based depth estimation**. I also work on occupancy prediction, weakly supervised semantic segmentation, and event-based vision. Alongside my publications, I have led and managed several government- and industry-funded research projects.

Research interests
